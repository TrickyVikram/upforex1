How OTC Stocks CFD Trading Works

When you enter a CFD stock trading contract, you are agreeing to exchange the difference in the value of a stock from the time the contract is opened to when it is closed. Instead of dealing in individual shares or pips, you trade in contract units that represent a specific quantity of shares. This efficient model eliminates the need for physical share exchange, streamlining the process while capturing the exact same price movement.
